SMuFL-Browser

A web based viewer for the Standard Music Font Layout specification

SMuFL

The Standard Music Font Layout (SMuFL) is a specification that provides a standard way of mapping the thousands of musical symbols required by conventional music notation into the Private Use Area in Unicode’s Basic Multilingual Plane.
